COMPANY PROFILE

Inaugurated in 2016 and headquartered in Pune, Maharashtra, "Agrovan" and "Unity Agro Foods" stand as two dynamic Companies being a part of Avenue Group of Companies. We take immense pride in our role as the primary manufacturer of an extensive range of Frozen, Canned, Dehydrated and spices, Fresh and Ready to eat. Our products are in high demand, celebrated for their exceptional quality, diverse designs, and budget-friendly pricing. What truly sets us apart is our unwavering commitment to delivering these outstanding products to our clients in a punctual manner. It's this dedication that has enabled us to cultivate a substantial and loyal client base within the market.

Our comprehensive approach covers the entire spectrum of our operations, encompassing farming, processing, storage, and the efficient distribution of our final products. This holistic strategy enables us to not only guarantee unwavering consistency but also to ensure an uninterrupted supply year-round. We have cultivated an extensive export network, effectively serving a broad clientele across the globe.

EXPORTS – FROM INDIA TO THE WORLD

Unity Agro Foods exports premium quality fruits and vegetables to leading retailers and manufacturers. Beyond bulk exports, we partner with global brands for Private Label solutions. Our own brands – Just Eat It, Klassik and Exotika – are expanding across the Middle East and new markets.
